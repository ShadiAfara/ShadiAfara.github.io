What Does Evaluate & Reflect Mean?

"Where students determine the credibility of sources, information, data, and ideas, then make their own research processes visible."

How it was applied in my work

The facet of research, “Evaluate and Reflect”, was used in our 3rd assignment (RWDA #3) were we were asked to critically evaluate the credibility of the resources we found in RWDA #2. We evaluated sources by investigating the content of the page, the structure of the page, the references, and even the design of the page. By completing this assignment, I learned how to evaluate a source and learned what information is valuable and what is not.
