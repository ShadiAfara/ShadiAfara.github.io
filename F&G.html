What Does Find & Generate Mean?

"Where students find information, and generate data/ideas using various methodologies."

How it was applied in my work

The nest assignment in WRIT 3988 (RWDA #2), asked us to investigate sources regarding various genres of writing and evaluating their information and credibility. In my assignment, I evaluated the similarities and differences between fictional and non-fictional writing styles. To do this, I used various search engines (Google, Yahoo, etc.) to locate my sources of information, then later compiled my findings into a more manageable form. During the assignment, I learned how to filter my findings, and how to display it to others in an easy-to-understand fashion.
