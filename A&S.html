What Does Analyse & Synthesise Mean?

"Where students analyse information/data critically and synthesise new knowledge to produce coherent individual/team understandings."

How it was applied in my work

After completing our draft of instructions in RWDA #4, we were given the opportunity to show them to our classmates and request feedback. RWDA #5 asked us to use the feedback provided to us to create our next edition. Not only did this assignment teach me what people look for in a set of instructions, but it also taught me what to look for in a good sample of instructional writing.
